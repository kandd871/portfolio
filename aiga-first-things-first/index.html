AIGA's First Things First, Second, & Third: A Manifesto Renewal
(print design) (risograph) (bookbinding)
In → Spring 2024
Under → Claudia Brandenburg
5 x 7 inches

An 8-page booklet for the AIGA exhibition titled First Things First, Second, and Third: a manifesto renewal, serving as an informational program for persons attending the exhibit. The booklet is printed using a risograph to invoke the feeling of something designed traditionally & with greater purpose than a corporate printer. It is made to feel personal through hand-drawn arrows & highlighted parts while signatures are placed in a more organic way.
