What blurs the line between digital & analog? I’m a multidisciplinary graphic designer, coder & artist from Bangalore, based in New York City. I’m currently a senior in the BFA Communication Design program at Parsons School of Design. My work revolves around exploring how interaction influences narrative.

From a very young age, I’ve had a passion for both science and art. My educational background until coming to college was completely STEM-based. Coding came naturally to me as I took it through high school, while art was purely a hobby. As for design, I didn’t even know how to use Photoshop until my senior year! Design is my perfect middle ground, allowing me to create innovative & analytic work. 

Reflective of my non-conventional advent into design, I’m interested in breaking conventions & creating non-traditional work. When I'm not designing, you can find me either painting, reading, dancing, napping, or yapping. *:･ﾟ✧*:･ﾟ☺
