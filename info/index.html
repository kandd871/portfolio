What blurs the line between digital & analog? Drithi Kandoor is a multidisciplinary graphic designer, coder & artist from Bangalore, based in New York City. Currently a senior in the BFA Communication Design program at Parsons School of Design, her work revolves around the exploration of how interaction influences narrative.

From a very young age, Drithi has had a passion for both science and art.Her educational background until coming to college was completely STEM-based. Coding came naturally to her as I took it through school, while art was purely a hobby. As for design, she didn’t even know how to use Photoshop until her senior year. Design serves as the perfect middle ground, allowing her to create innovative & analytic work. 

Reflective of her non-conventional advent into design, she's interested in breaking conventions & creating non-traditional work. When she's not designing, you can find her painting, reading, dancing, or taking a nap.
