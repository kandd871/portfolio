Drithi Kandoor is a multidisciplinary graphic designer, coder & artist from Bangalore, now based in New York City. She recently graduated from Parsons School of Design with a BFA in Communication Design and is now designing at Pentagram.


She's interested in the intersection of digital & analog, with work that often centers around code, interaction, illustration, and typography. Passionate about breaking conventions, she hopes to create non-traditional work that blends design with art, science, and technology. When she's not designing, you can find her painting, reading, dancing, or taking a nap.
