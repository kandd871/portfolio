Mutated Odyssey
(print design) (typography) (branding) (bookbinding)
In → Spring 2024
Under → Claudia Brandenburg
Includes 4½ x 10½ in. Catalog

Catalog design & branding for an imaginary exhibition for the artist Rajni Perera at Karma Gallery, NYC. The catalog can be flipped & read either way – one side tells you about the artist & the other tells you about the exhibition. The duality of the orientation & colors represents the duality & experimentation shown in her work & the typography reflects the theme of science fiction which she uses a source of inspiration.
